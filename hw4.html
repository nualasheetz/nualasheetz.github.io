City College San Francisco

City College is often referred to by its initials, CCSF. It is a true community college that serves a very diverse student body. Students can earn an associates degree in science or arts. They can take classes that prepare them to transfer to a California State College or University. Students can also earn certificates to show their mastery of important job skills. City College helps to improve the job readiness of our citizens and provides essential training for our essential workers including nurses, auto mechanics, and fire-fighters. City College also provides noncredit classes in basic skills and English as a Second Language. You can also take classes to prepare for the citizenship exam. City College makes our community stronger by fostering educated citizens and skilled workers.
